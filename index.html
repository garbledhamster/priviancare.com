Our Commitment to Compassionate, RN-Led Support

Mission Statement

Privian Care Management is dedicated to enhancing seniors' safety, independence, and well-being through compassionate, RN-led support. We empower families through clarity, advocacy, and personalized care grounded in dignity and respect.

Values & Philosophy

We embrace whole-person care by blending clinical expertise with genuine human connection. Seniors deserve to feel understood, supported, and respected — and families deserve guidance they can trust.

Our Objectives

We aim to deliver safe, RN-guided care, reduce stress for families through clear coordination, promote independence through proactive monitoring, and advocate for seniors across healthcare settings.

Service Area

We proudly serve seniors within approximately a 1-hour drive of Minneapolis.
