Services

RN-led

RN Care Management

Care coordination, advocacy, and clarity—focused on safety, continuity, and communication.

  • Monthly wellness check-ins and oversight
  • Medication list clarity and coordination
  • Appointments: scheduling + follow-through
  • Family updates (email/text/call)

Concierge

Senior Concierge Support

Practical help that keeps the household moving—respectfully, privately, and reliably.

  • Errands, groceries, and logistics
  • Light household support & organization
  • Meal prep / routines support
  • Paperwork help (forms, calls, scheduling)
  • Tech basics (phone/iPad reminders)
Structured

Monthly Check-In Visit

A consistent RN touchpoint to notice changes early and reduce uncertainty.

  • Health & mood observations
  • Medication overview (non-diagnostic)
  • Home environment & safety notes
  • Routine and support recommendations
  • Plain-language family summary

A simple process that reduces stress fast

A clear, steady rhythm—so families get updates, seniors get support, and everyone relaxes a little.

1) Quick call (calm + practical)

We learn what’s happening, what matters most, and what would help right now.

2) First visit (baseline + safety)

A structured check-in to establish routines, safety awareness, and communication preferences.

3) Care plan (simple next steps)

Appointments, coordination, routines, and home supports—broken into doable steps.

4) Ongoing support + updates

Consistent check-ins, practical help, and updates—so families stay aligned and informed.
