Our Commitment to Compassionate, RN-Led Support

Mission Statement

Privian Care Management is dedicated to enhancing seniors' safety, independence, and well-being through compassionate, RN-led guidance. Our mission is to support both clients and families with expert advocacy, clear communication, and customized care rooted in dignity and respect.

Values & Philosophy

Our approach embraces whole-person care. We combine clinical expertise with genuine human connection, ensuring each client feels seen, understood, and supported. Our philosophy is simple: care should be safe, warm, person-centered, and guided by evidence-based nursing practices.

Our Objectives

We aim to deliver high-quality, safe RN-guided care; reduce stress for families through clear coordination; promote independence through proactive wellness monitoring; and advocate for seniors across healthcare environments to ensure their needs are heard and met.

Service Area

We proudly serve seniors within approximately a 1-hour drive of the Minneapolis metro area.
